Mars Images

This website shows images taken from Mars from the rovers(Opportunity,Spirit,Curiosity).

The term “Sol” is short for Solar day, which is the time it takes Mars to revolve once around its own axis.

Curiosity Rover- 0-2105 SOL and Active,Opportunity Rover-0-5107 SOL and Compleated,Spirit Rover-0-2208 SOL and Compleated
